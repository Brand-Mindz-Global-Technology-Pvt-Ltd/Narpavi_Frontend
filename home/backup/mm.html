Everything You Need to Know

+

Is Narpavai Honey 100% pure and natural?

Yes, Narpavai Honey is 100% pure, unprocessed, and free from any additives or preservatives. We source our honey directly from trusted beekeepers to ensure natural goodness in every drop.
+

How do I store honey properly?

Store honey in a tightly sealed container at room temperature, away from direct sunlight and moisture. Avoid refrigeration to maintain its natural consistency.
+

Is your honey safe for kids and elders?

Absolutely! Our honey is 100% natural and safe for all ages. However, it’s not recommended for infants below one year old.
+

Does honey expire or crystallize over time?

Honey never truly expires. Over time, it may crystallize naturally due to its sugar content. Gently warm it to return it to its smooth liquid form.
